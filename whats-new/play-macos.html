What’s New in Play

Version 2.0.6

Released Jan 27, 2024


• Tags now show their associated folder names across the app.PLAY PREMIUM

• Play should now be able to recognize all installed browsers and allow you to select your preferred one. Configure your preferred browser in Preferences → Browser.

• You can now adjust the sorting order directly from the toolbar.

Version 2.0.5

Released Jan 10, 2024


• Added the ability to watch new videos from your channels inline, without having to first save them for later.PLAY PREMIUM

• The Get Videos shortcut action now supports filtering by multiple tags.

• You can now view the complete video title in the list of videos saved for later. Configure in Preferences → UI.

• Added a new Play Selected on YouTube menu item to play all selected videos at once.

• Fixed a crash when trying to set the Start At attribute on videos that have a 0:00 length.

Version 2.0.4

Released Dec 12, 2023


• Interactive widget for tracking newly released videos from channels you follow.PLAY PREMIUM

• Added the ability to keep the video player window always on top. Configure in Preferences → UI.

• Added support for the play://channels URL scheme to quickly open the Channels Inbox.PLAY PREMIUM

• Added Date Watched as a possible attribute for filtering and sorting videos in the Find Video shortcut action.

• Fix: When the Mark as Watched parameter is enabled, selecting Play All New on YouTube will now accurately play the selected tag and smart search.

• Fix: The Channels Inbox status bar now converts the elapsed time into hours if it has been more than 60 minutes since the last update.

Version 2.0.3

Released Dec 6, 2023


• Follow channels from your existing videos in Channels → control-click (+) → Add Existing Channels...PLAY PREMIUM

• Configure a custom Piped/Invidious instance to watch videos.

• Keep up with all the latest updates with the new What’s New in Play document, available in Help → What’s New.

Version 2.0.2

Version 2.0.1

Released Nov 27, 2023


• Playing all new videos now gives you the option to watch without marking them as watched when the Mark as Watched parameter is active.

• Fix: Importing videos from a playlist no longer duplicates existing videos.

Version 2.0.0

Released Nov 27, 2023


Play 2.0 brings the ability to follow YouTube channels and get new videos as they're released. You can save the ones you want to watch, and delete the rest. It integrates harmoniously with the existing feature set, allowing you to have a complete YouTube experience within the app - get new videos, save, organize, and watch without algorithms!


It also adds the ability to create folders for Tags and Smart Searches, giving you more ways to catalog and organize your videos.


These features are part of Play Premium, a subscription designed for features that extend Play's capabilities beyond saving videos for later. It brings a more sustainable business model to the app and allows me to invest in more advanced features, ensuring the app's long-term development.
