Kiwi Queue privacy policy

This policy applies to all information collected or submitted on Kiwi Queue app for iPhone and any other devices and platforms.

Information we collect

When opening the app for the first time, you have to login to your YouTube Account. This will be used to fetch new videos from channels you are subscribed. Any information regarding your account is kept on the device and is not shared with anyone.

We store your YouTube token on the device, so we can use it to fetch new videos.

We may collect anonymous statistics such as how many times the app was opened.

Information usage

The collected information is not shared with outside parties, except to the extent necessary to accomplish the app's functionality. For example, we can send your YouTube token to Google to authenticate you.

The only information we have on our servers is the anonymous statistics we collect. Everything else is kept on your device.

Accessing, changing, or deleting your information

As every information collected is kept on device, deleting the app will delete the collected information.

Third-party links and content

Kiwi Queue has links to YouTube content. They have their own independent privacy policies, and we have no responsibility or liability for their content or activities.

Your Consent

By using our app, you consent to our privacy policy.
