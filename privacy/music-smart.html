MusicSmart privacy policy

Information collected

When opening the app for the first time, you can choose to authorize access to your music library. This will be used to show the list of albums you have in your library. No personal information is collected, or uploaded to any server.

The app keeps locally the store location you're associated with, so it can search and find albums using the correct store. None of this information is sent to any server on the internet, it is kept on your device, and used to enhance your experience while using the app.

Accessing, changing, or deleting your information

As the only user-information stored is the music store location, and is stored locally on the user's device, deleting the app will delete this information.
