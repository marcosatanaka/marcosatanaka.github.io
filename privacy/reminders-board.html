Reminders Board privacy policy

Information collected

Access to your Reminders database is required for Reminders Board to be useful. It will be used to show your reminders and lists, and to enable you to modify them. All information available to Reminders Board is kept on your device, and it's not shared with anyone.

Accessing, changing, or deleting your information

As Reminders Board uses the Reminders database, users can delete the data in Reminders to remove it from Reminders Board.

Your Consent

By using our app, you consent to our privacy policy.
