Bringing Liquid Glass and Apple Intelligence to My Apps

@mactanaka September 01, 2025

I think it’s fair to say that this year’s WWDC had an impact on nearly every iOS app. Not only did we get a redesign of the entire system, but Apple also introduced the Foundation Model APIs, unlocking a whole new range of possibilities with Apple Intelligence.

It took me a while to figure out exactly what I wanted to do with my apps for this release, but once it clicked, everything started to come together naturally. I’m really excited for users to get these updates when iOS 26 comes out in a few days!

In the meantime, I wanted to share my thoughts and experiences on integrating Liquid Glass and Apple Intelligence, and what these changes mean for users:

  • MusicSmart
