About SCO.re

Manage your portfolio and invest more wisely with the help of SCO.re.


SCO.re generates a score for each company based on the user's list of criteria. Use it to find the stocks that best meet your standards.


Users can update their companies as quarterly or annual results are released and check their updated scores to decide whether to continue investing in them.

Core features

  • Add companies, groups/individual criteria, and tags.
  • Import companies and groups of criteria from a text file.
  • Quickly access news and investor relations websites of your companies.
  • Add tags for quick classification and filtering.
  • Use a robust set of filters to find the company you are looking for easily.
  • Your data is securely synced between devices with the same iCloud account.
  • Add parametrizable widgets for each company to your Home Screen.

Pricing

SCO.re is free to download and use with up to three companies. After that, users can purchase a single in-app purchase of $2.99 to evaluate an unlimited number of companies and change the app icon. It is a universal app for iPhone and iPad and requires iOS/iPadOS 15.
