MusicHarbor press kit

MusicHarbor was created to help people who want to keep up with the latest releases from artists they love. It's integrated with Apple Music so you can import artists from your library.

The idea is simple: the app monitore artists you like and notify you when they release something new. It also provides a feed of albums releases, chronologically ordered, so you can have a list of new releases really tailored to what you follow.

So in June 2018 I began to work on the app, and the first version was released in November. And I'm constantly updating it to add requested features and improve stability and performance.

Features

  • Feed of releases from artists you follow
  • Import artists you have in your Apple Music library
  • Receive notification when a new album is released
  • Filter releases by album, single, EP and remix
  • New: check out the 61st GRAMMY© Awards nominees
