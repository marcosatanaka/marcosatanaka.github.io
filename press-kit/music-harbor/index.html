MusicHarbor press kit

From someone who always missed the release of new music and relied on lists of top 100 songs to know what's new, comes a simple solution called MusicHabor.

The idea is simple: the app monitor artists you like, and then notify you when they release something new. It also provides a feed of albums releases, chronologically ordered, so you can have a list of new releases really tailored to what you follow.

So in June 2018 I began to work on the app. And now it is finally ready to ship! There are many other things to do, but I can ship these as updates to the app.

Features

  • Feed of releases from artists you follow
  • Import artists you have in your Apple Music library
  • Receive notification when a new album is released
  • Filter releases by album, single, EP and remix
