About MusicBox

MusicBox is a flexible listen later app for Apple Music, Spotify, Deezer, and Bandcamp. You can use it to:

  • Keep track of new music you want to listen to later.
  • Catalog and organize favorite albums and songs.
  • Categorize music with tags, assign notes, manage metadata, and more.

MusicBox collects relevant metadata of albums and songs and is deeply integrated into the system with features such as Widgets, Share Sheet, Shortcuts, iCloud, and more.


Core Features

MusicBox is a modern app that supports the latest system features: Home and Lock Screen widgets, Shortcuts, Action Extension, Context Menu, iCloud sync, dark mode, and more. It is available for the iPhone, iPad, and Mac.

  • Multiple streaming services: MusicBox supports albums and songs from Apple Music, Spotify, Deezer, and Bandcamp.
  • Easy to add: Add music from the Share Sheet, via drag and drop, with Shazam, or by importing from a playlist.
  • Tags: Apply tags to categorize your music and make it easier to find.
  • Auto-Tagging: Automatically tag albums and songs based on rules you set.
  • Deep Search: Search your music by attributes such as title, artist, genre, record label, tags, type, and notes.
  • Smart Search: Automatically filter albums and songs based on a set of rules.
  • Focus Filter: Configure a Focus mode to limit the videos and tags shown in Play.
  • Shortcuts: Automate workflows using MusicBox's actions and data.
  • Widgets: Access your albums and songs directly from your Home and Lock Screen.
  • Shazam: Easily bookmark music playing around you.
  • iCloud: Your database is automatically synced to your iPhone, iPad, and Mac.
  • Mac: MusicBox is also available on the Mac.

Price and Availability

MusicBox costs $2.99 and doesn't have any in-app purchases or subscriptions. It is a universal app for iPhone, iPad, and Mac.


MusicBox requires iOS/iPadOS 15 and macOS Monterey.
