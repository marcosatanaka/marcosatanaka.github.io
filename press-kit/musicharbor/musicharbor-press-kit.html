About MusicHarbor

MusicHarbor is an app that helps you stay on top of new music releases, music videos, events, and news from all your favorite artists and record labels.


It does that by integrating with your Apple Music and Spotify and by scanning all the artists from your library. You can follow these artists and labels in the app, and it will aggregate a chronologically ordered list of new music releases for you.


You don't need an Apple Music or Spotify account to use the app, though, as it supports adding artists and record labels by manually searching as well.

Check out everything new in MusicHarbor for iOS 17, iPadOS 17, and macOS Sonoma!

Core Features

  • Track everything: new and upcoming music releases, videos, interviews and live performances, events, artists' news, and album reviews.
  • Import artists: Apple Music, Spotify, Last.fm, and your local music library.
  • Integrate with your music streaming service: add songs to your Apple Music or Spotify library and playlists. Open albums in the Music and Spotify app, or dozens of different services using Songlink integration.
  • Filter releases by type: choose which kinds of releases you want to know about: full albums, singles, EPs, remixes, live performances, and acoustic albums.
  • Widgets: see the latest and upcoming releases right from your Home and Lock Screen.
  • Shortcuts: manage your MusicHarbor library using custom actions in the Shortcuts app, including support for predicate filtering for finding albums, artists, music videos, and actions to change the app's settings.
  • iCloud Syncing: your artists, record labels, and releases are synced to all iOS, iPadOS, and macOS devices using the same iCloud account.
  • iPhone, iPad, and Mac: you can use MusicHarbor on your favorite device because it is available on both App Store and Mac App Store with optimized versions for each platform.
  • Support for modern technologies: MusicHarbor is constantly updated and supports the newest iOS, iPadOS, and macOS features, such as Home and Lock Screen widgets, App Shortcuts, Swift Charts, cursor and keyboard support, context menus, spotlight search, iCloud sync, dynamic type, and more.
  • Privacy-focused: you don't need an account to use the app, and all your data is processed locally on the device.

About the Development

MusicHarbor started as a simple iPhone app to track new music releases on Apple Music. Since version 1.0, it has gained many features and become a complete solution for music enthusiasts who want to be on top of new music releases, music videos, events, and news from their favorite artists and record labels.
