MusicHarbor

App Store featured app that integrates with Apple Music and enables you to track new music releases, music videos, concert dates and news from your favorite artists.


"If you care about new music releases and staying on top of your favorite artists, you need MusicHarbor." - Federico Viticci (editor-in-chief, MacStories)


"This is a great app for folks who are trying to find new music from their favorite artists." - Mikah Sargent (host, iOS Today - TWiT network)

Features

Releases, concerts and news

Explore new music and video releases in a unified, chronologically ordered, timeline. Know about concerts and festivals. Read the latest news from your favorite artists.

Apple Music

Import artists from your Apple Music library (or add them manually). Add songs to your playlists or listen to them using your Apple Music account.

Unlimited

Follow unlimited artists without restrictions. You can also customize your experience, by buying a one-time IAP to unlock extra features.

Siri Shortcuts and Spotlight

Ask Siri about the latest news from your favorite artists. Or find them using Spotlight on your phone.

iCloud

Sync the artists you follow between all your iOS devices.

Dark mode

Fully integrated with iOS 13 dark mode.
